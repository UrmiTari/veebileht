Why this webpage was created: This site was created by two students, Urmi and Armas, from University of Tartu as a project to learn and practice the usage of HTML, CSS etc.

Camera: Majority of the pictures are taken with an Huawei P30 Lite phone camera (not sponsored by Huawei 😀), around Pärnu and Tartu. Mostly showing off the natural beauty of Estonia with its rich and beautiful nature. Pictures haven’t been modified in any way apart from the phones own night mode and general cropping of the pictures.

Additionally neither one of us had prior experience with HTML, CSS and JavaScript. So this project has been rather interesting and good learning experience. 😀
